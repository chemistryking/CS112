Section 1: Edit Item on Schedule

You can the edit feature of LifeManager to edit items that have previously been added to your schedule.

Imagine that you want to edit any part of an item on your schedule. The detailed steps in this section show you how to:
	1. initiate the editing process,
	2. change the item information,
	3. save any changes to schedule.

Step 1:
	Tap on the Schedule tab on the bottom left of the screen.

Step 2:
	Tap on the Schedule item that you wish to change.

Step 3:
	Tap on any information that you wish to change and change it.

Step 4:
	Tap on the "Save Changes" button.

Now you are done!
